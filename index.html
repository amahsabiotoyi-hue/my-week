Non-negotiables

Progress saves on this device automatically and each day gets its own checklist. A perfect day = every box ticked.

The contract (read this when you want to skip)

1
No zero-action days. Every business block ends with something shipped — a message sent, a call made, a page published, a person asked. Notes alone don't count.
2
Never miss twice. Missing one day is an accident; missing two is the start of a new (bad) habit.
3
Shrink it, don't skip it. Exhausted? Do the 15-minute version. The streak matters more than any single session's length.
4
Phone sleeps outside the bedroom. The 4:30 wake-up is won at the 10:00 PM lights-out.
5
Sunday review, every Sunday. Score the week 1–10, count shipped actions, set next week's #1 goal.

2-week business sprint

Week 1 — Pick & validate
  • Days 1–2: Brain-dump every idea. Score: excites you / people pay for it / startable with < $200. Keep top 3.
  • Days 3–5: Validate — talk to 5 potential customers per idea, check competitor pricing, estimate first-month cost.
  • Days 6–7: Choose ONE. Define the smallest sellable version.
Week 2 — Ship
  • Days 8–10: Build the minimum: landing page, offer doc, or first package. Ugly is fine; done is the goal.
  • Days 11–12: Put the offer in front of 10 real people.
  • Days 13–14: First sale attempt. Adjust price/pitch. Log every response.

Python restart (after the 3-month pause)

Don't restart from zero. Days 1–3: replay the finished half at 1.5–2× and redo one exercise per module. Day 4+: new material. Lunch breaks: optional 20-min drills.

Honest note on sleep

10:00 PM → 4:30 AM is 6.5 hours. Weekend sleep-ins are the recovery valve. Dragging after two weeks? Move wake-up to 5:00 before dropping any block.
